# Music credits and rights notes

Zoomies uses four independently licensed songs as synchronized PlayStation XA
channels.

All four shipping masters preserve the complete source song at least once in
each 256-second cycle. No track is pitch-shifted, time-stretched, compressed, or
limited. The build applies static loudness gain, removes subsonic DC content,
resamples to 37,800 Hz stereo, arranges an authored loop extension where needed,
and encodes the result as 8-bit PlayStation XA ADPCM. These are modifications
under CC BY 4.0.

## Graphics level 1: Malt Shop Bop

- Track and artist: **Malt Shop Bop**, Kevin MacLeod.
- Official source: [Incompetech track page](https://incompetech.com/music/royalty-free/index.html?isrc=USUAN1100496), ISRC `USUAN1100496`.
- License: [Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/).
- Required attribution: `"Malt Shop Bop" Kevin MacLeod (incompetech.com)`.
- Zoomies adaptation: complete 166.087-second song followed by a bar-aligned
  loop bed; static gain, 20 Hz high-pass, resampling, dithering, and XA encoding.
